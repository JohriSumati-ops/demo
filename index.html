Stream 01 · Science Non-Medical

PCM ⚙️

Maximum scope, hardest path. JEE is the gateway — but not the only door.

Key Exams
⚡
JEE Main
Gateway to NITs, IIITs, GFTIs. 2 attempts/year. Highest competition in India.
🏆
JEE Advanced
IITs only. Top 2.5L qualify. Most prestigious engineering exam in India.
🎯
BITSAT
BITS Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad. Online. Faster, predictable results.
🔬
IISER IAT
Research path. BSc-MS dual degree. Monthly stipend available.
⚡ Smart Strategy
Apply JEE → JoSAA, JAC Delhi, IPU all together. Add BITSAT early. Keep IISER as research backup. Never bet on one exam!
Career Paths
💻
Engineering/Tech
JEE Main · JEE Adv · BITSAT · VITEEE
🔬
Pure Science
IISER IAT · NEST · ISI · CMI
📊
Data Science/Econ
CUET UG · IPMAT · ISI
🪖
Defense/NDA
NDA · TES (via JEE Main)
Counselling Portals
🌍
JoSAA
IITs, NITs, IIITs. 6+ rounds. Choice filling is everything.
🏙️
JAC Delhi
DTU, NSUT, IIIT Delhi. 85% seats for Delhi students.
🏢
IPU Counselling
USICT, MAIT, MSIT. More seats, easier CS entry.
⚡
BITS Pilani
BITSAT score. Own portal. Earlier than JEE counselling.
Stream 02 · Science Medical

PCB 🧬

Focused path. NEET is primary — but smart students always keep backups ready.

Key Exams
🩺
NEET UG
MBBS, BDS, BAMS. 720 marks. One attempt per year. Extremely competitive.
🔬
IISER IAT
Research alternative. Excellent stipend. BS-MS in life sciences.
📋
CUET UG
DU, BHU, JNU — Psychology, Bio, Biotech. Essential backup.
🌾
ICAR AIEEA
Agriculture, Forestry, Food Tech. High-value career path.
⚡ Smart Strategy
Primary: NEET. Backup: CUET UG + IISER IAT. Register CUET before NEET results. AIQ (MCC) + State counselling run separately — apply to both!
Counselling Systems
🏛️
MCC (AIQ)
15% all-India quota + AIIMS + Deemed. Rounds: R1, R2, Mop-up.
🗺️
State Counselling
85% state quota. Run separately. Apply to each eligible state.
🎓
CUET Portals
No central system — DU, BHU, JNU each have own portals.
🔬
IISER / NISER
Separate merit-based admission for research institutes.
Stream 03 · Business & Finance

Commerce 📊

Most underrated stream. Highest ROI. Multi-application is the entire strategy.

Key Exams
🏛️
IPMAT
IIM Indore/Rohtak. 5-year integrated MBA. Very competitive, limited seats.
💰
CA Foundation
Chartered Accountancy. Start anytime after 12th. No entrance needed.
📋
CUET UG
DU (SRCC, Hindu), BHU, JNU. BCom Hons, Economics, BBA.
⚖️
CLAT
24 NLUs. 5-year integrated law. Corporate law = very high ROI.
⚡ Smart Strategy
Apply: CUET + IPMAT + CA Foundation + 2-3 private colleges — simultaneously. Unlike PCM there's no single portal. Track all separately and never miss a deadline.
Counselling Systems
🎓
CUET / DU CSAS
BCom, Economics, BBA. Delhi University central portal.
⚖️
CLAT Counselling
Centralized for all 24 NLUs. Very competitive, high ROI.
🏛️
IPMAT Counselling
IIM Indore/Rohtak separate portals. 5-year MBA pathway.
🏢
Private Colleges
Symbiosis, Christ, NMIMS — own portals & tests.
Stream 04 · Arts & Social Sciences

Humanities 📚

Most flexible, most misunderstood. Strategy-driven, not exam-driven. Profile matters here.

Key Exams
⚖️
CLAT
24 NLUs. ₹20L+ packages at top firms. Centralized counselling.
🎓
CUET UG
200+ universities. Psychology, History, Political Science, Sociology.
📡
IIMC
Premier journalism institute. Very competitive, high industry value.
🎨
NID DAT / NIFT

Apply: CUET (DU + BHU + others) + CLAT + IPU + 2-3 private universities. For Ashoka/FLAME/Krea: extracurriculars + writing + interviews matter more than marks. Build your profile now.

⚖️
CLAT Counselling
Centralized for all NLUs. Must apply to all 24 simultaneously.
🎓

History, Pol Sci, Psychology. Apply through DU portal.
🎨
NID/NIFT Counselling
Portfolio + test. Centralized admission.
🌟
Liberal Arts Unis
Ashoka, FLAME, Krea — profile-based. Apply early.

🔑 The Golden Rule of Counselling
Same rank, different outcome. The student who masters counselling beats the student with 10 more marks. Apply to ALL eligible portals simultaneously. Never wait for one result to apply to the next. A seat held at a lower-preference college can always be upgraded.
